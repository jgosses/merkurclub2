Merkur Club of America

National Events

Carlisle Ford Nationals

June 4 - June 6, 2021

Carlisle, PA

The largest gathering of Merkur vehicles. Convoy to the show with your regional Merkur friends and join us for a weekend of driving, swapping (stories and/or parts), and inspiration for your next project.

Hotel Accomodations

Merkur Club of America members receive a discounted group rate at:

Hotel Radisson

Camp Hill, PA

The staple of the Merkurs at Carlisle experience can be found in the hotel parking lot. Line up the cars out back and enjoy!

3-Step Carlisle Signup Process:

Step One

Register with Carlisle Events

Be sure to indicate "Merkur Club of America" to be included in the club attendance count!

Step Two

Reserve your hotel room

Step Three

Renew your Merkur Club of America membership

The MCA, together with our major sponsors, will be handing out the awards from the show-field judging earlier in the day, as well as selecting the winners of the prizes donated by our generous sponsors. Your MCA membership number will serve as your ticket for the drawing, so be sure to renew your membership prior to the show!

For more information, visit the Carlisle Frequently Asked Questions page.

For directions and Merkur-sponsored routes, visit the Carlisle Directions page.

To join or organize a driving convoy to Carlisle, please post in the Carlisle 2020 forum or send an email to directors@merkurclub.com to coordinate with other MCA members. Over the years, there have been convoys from the west along I-70 and I-76 and from the Northern Virginia area through Maryland, among others. Convoys are a great way to get to know your fellow MCA members and arrive to the showfield and hotel in style.
