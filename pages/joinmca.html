Join and/or Renew MCA Membership

If you are unsure if your membership is current or expired, just drop an e-mail to us requesting your membership renewal date. There is no issue renewing prior to the actual expiration date; the time will just be added on.

Anyone over the age of 18 can join, whether or not you are a current Merkur owner.

Annual Individual membership dues are only $20.00 or two years pre-paid for $35. You may select either option from the PayPal on-line order form linked below. Past or current members renewing their membership should make the appropriate selection from the "renew" options, so that we'll know to continue using your existing information in the member database. The MCA membership year goes from April 1st through March 31st of the following year. Two-year sign-ups will simply be extended for an additional year.

Enter your name, email address, and vehicle information in the corresponding text box to the membership you'd like to purchase, then click 'Add To Cart' to purchase through PayPal.
